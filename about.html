2021 International Legislative Stakeholders Conference on Cybersecurity.

The Conference seeks to create an interface between ICT and Cybercrime sectors to debate how ICT can be leveraged at scale to support the achievement of the past. The Conference outputs will include a high-level Statement to provide stakeholders with policy recommendations about how to harness the power of ICT to address current cyber security challenges and to ensure equitable quality awareness opportunities for all, and following up actions and relevant partnerships that are aimed to help stakeholders develop system-wide ICT and cyber security awareness strategies.
